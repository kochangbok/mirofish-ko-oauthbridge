# Korean market translation path and sector sensitivity

## Translation path
1. Hormuz tension / support announcement
2. higher expected oil, KRW weakness, war-risk insurance discussion
3. foreign futures selling and KOSPI heavyweight de-rating language
4. retail communities generate defense / shipbuilding / refining watchlists
5. intraday re-evaluation if officials clarify or oil stabilizes

## Base sector hypotheses
- defense: first upside narrative candidate
- shipbuilding / maritime security: stronger when paired with defense
- shipping: mixed because freight upside and route disruption fear coexist
- refining: can spike early but remain highly unstable
- airlines: first downside narrative candidate
- petrochemicals: feedstock and cost pressure
- semiconductors: likely to carry a large share of index downside pressure
- autos / appliances: FX benefit and risk-off pressure collide
