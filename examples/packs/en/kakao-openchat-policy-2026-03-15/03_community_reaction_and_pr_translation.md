# Community reaction and PR translation path

## Diffusion path
1. internal screenshot leaks
2. DC Inside / Blind / X redistribute the most provocative lines
3. reporters and tech YouTubers package the controversy into digestible summaries
4. Kakao posts the official notice, but the leaked interpretation is already the baseline frame
5. the follow-up clarification either lands as apology or as corporate excuse

## Baseline hypotheses by group
- room operators: likely strongest backlash
- anonymous worker communities: highly sensitive to anonymity loss
- casual users: may respond through inconvenience and vague unease rather than legal/privacy detail
- reporters / YouTubers: compress the conflict into a few viral lines
- regulators / politicians: may pull the issue into youth safety vs overregulation debates
