# Owner community, regulator, and competitor reaction

## Diffusion path
1. overseas clips spread
2. Korean auto communities compress the story into “fire” and “battery danger” language
3. Tesla Korea issues the recall announcement
4. owner communities defend it as early intervention
5. the transport ministry launches a review
6. Hyundai/Kia beneficiary talk competes with broader EV distrust

## Baseline hypotheses by group
- Tesla owner communities: likely to defend via service experience and OTA capability
- general auto communities: likely to amplify ridicule and danger framing
- regulators / experts: likely to demand clearer fault explanation and scope
- competitor fans / investors: likely to manufacture Hyundai/Kia beneficiary talk
