# Recall background and brand-trust risk

## Why the narrative gets large
- In EV discourse, the words battery, safety, and fire can destabilize an entire technology-brand narrative.
- More important than recall size is whether Tesla appears fast, candid, and generous in its remedy.
- Owners tend to defend the brand; non-owners tend to generalize the risk.

## Interpretation guide
1. the first six hours are dominated by clips, screenshots, and community summaries
2. if the recall wording is too technical or vague, distrust grows
3. “preventive” can read either as responsibility or as evasion
4. once regulators enter, the issue can expand from product quality into institutional trust
