# Scenario memo
**Topic:** How owner communities, general auto communities, regulators, and competitor narratives collide after Tesla Korea announces a large Model Y / Model 3 recall
**Anchor:** 2026-03-15 18:00 KST

## 1. Separate context from injected assumptions
### Real-world context
- In EV controversies, brand trust often depends less on the defect itself than on speed, transparency, and remedy.
- Korean auto communities usually show a large gap between owner and non-owner reactions.
- Investor narratives and consumer narratives can move in very different directions.

### Injected simulation assumptions
- Tesla Korea announces at 18:00 KST a large recall for some Model Y and Model 3 vehicles due to a possible battery temperature sensor issue.
- During the prior 12 hours, short X and Reddit clips that look like smoke from parked cars spread quickly.
- The transport ministry announces a safety review the next morning.
- Tesla calls it a preventive measure, while some users interpret it as a delayed admission.
