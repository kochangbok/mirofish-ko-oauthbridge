# Prompt examples — Kakao Open Chat policy backlash

## Prompt 1 — leaked draft vs official notice
Using the uploaded seed, simulate user and operator reactions from six hours before the leak to 24 hours after the official notice. Separate DC Inside, Blind, Naver Cafe, X, reporters, and tech YouTubers.

## Prompt 2 — framing contest
Compare whether Kakao’s explanation lands as “crime prevention” or turns into “privacy invasion and the collapse of anonymity.” Include backlash intensity (0~100) and likely churn intent by stakeholder group.

## Prompt 3 — follow-up clarification test
Assume Kakao publishes a follow-up clarification 12 hours later. Propose five phrases that reduce backlash and five phrases that make the “expanded surveillance” frame worse.

## Prompt 4 — structured output version
Answer in this format: A. five-line summary B. community-by-community reaction table C. timeline after the leak D. churn / migration risk E. PR rewrite suggestions.
